PredictionMarketPulse.com ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-BQGMFEFQJ1) to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page

  • Browser type, device, and operating system

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. Your IP address is anonymized before it is stored. We do not use Google Analytics to track individual users or to collect personally identifiable information.

Opt-out: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

Cookies

We use cookies solely for the purposes of Google Analytics (as described above) and to remember your dark mode preference. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from third-party ad networks.

Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Name — to address you in our response

Contact form submissions are processed through Netlify Forms. Your data is stored securely by Netlify and is accessible only to us. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and will not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months, after which they are deleted unless an ongoing business relationship has been established.

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Data collected through Google Analytics is processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Contact form data is processed by Netlify in accordance with Netlify's Privacy Policy.
